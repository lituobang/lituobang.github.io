ALR-CA基本规则 / ALR-CA Core Rules Summary

1. 时间分片集合竞价 / Time-Slotted Auction

模块化清算：全天划分为多个离散的竞价模块，定时进行集合竞价清算。

Slotted Clearing: The day is divided into multiple discrete auction modules, with scheduled collective clearing performed.

2. 报价与匹配逻辑 / Bidding & Matching

义务与优先权：成功匹配的订单必须按均衡价履行交易，且享有最高级别的派单与结算优先权。职业参与者可挂设长期单以提供基础流动性。

Obligation & Priority: Matched orders are mandatory to fulfill at the equilibrium price and hold top-tier priority. Professional participants may place standing orders to provide base liquidity.

3. 稳健定价保障 / Robust Pricing

分级处理：流动性充足时执行稳定清算；流动性不足时自动通过历史价格加权平滑以防止价格畸变。

Tier-based Logic: Tier 1 (Stable) clearing for high liquidity; Tier 2 (Fragile) invokes historical weighting to prevent price distortion in thin markets.

4. 开放成交机制 / Overflow Execution

溢出成交：未在清算时匹配的参与者若愿意接受已确定的均衡价，只要市场仍有容量，可按该价格后续成交。根据清算时的报价高低享有优先排队权，但不保证可以成交。

Open Fulfillment: Unmatched participants willing to accept the finalized equilibrium price may still execute trades as long as market capacity remains. Priority is granted based on the price level of bids submitted during the auction, but fulfillment is not guaranteed.

建议 / Strategic Recommendations

  • 流动性激励 / Incentives: 建议对提供长期流动性的职业参与者实施“平台佣金减免”。 / Recommend commission discounts for long-term liquidity providers.
  • 动态阈值 / Adaptive τ: 建议根据高峰/低谷时段动态调整流动性系数τ。 / Suggest dynamically adjusting liquidity coefficient τ based on peak/off-peak periods.
  • 公共流动性储备 / Public Liquidity Reserve: 在长期流动性短缺的市场（如网约车市场）中，政府应组织大型企事业提供基础流动性支撑。 / In markets with chronic liquidity shortage, such as ride-hailing, the government should organize large enterprises to provide baseline liquidity.
  • 集体抱团报价 / Collective Demand Pooling: 在外卖市场中，需求不稳定的中小型商家可以组成“需求联盟”共同下单。当某一家商铺订单不足时，联盟内其他商铺的冗余订单可以共享该清算头寸，从而确保整体需求的稳定性并降低违约风险。 / In the delivery market, small merchants with volatile demand can form "Demand Alliances" to bid collectively. If one merchant lacks orders, the redundant demand from others within the alliance fills the slot, ensuring overall stability and minimizing default risks.
  • 订单无条件转让 / Unconditional Order Transfer: 商家在清算后若发现实际单量低于预期，可将已匹配的需求订单在二级市场进行无条件转让。这允许供给在商家之间二次分配，避免浪费。 / Merchants can unconditionally transfer their matched positions in a secondary market if actual order volume is lower than expected. This enables the redistribution of supply among merchants and prevents the waste.
